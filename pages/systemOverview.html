About INPRS

With approximately $46.7 billion in assets under management at fiscal year-end 2023, the Indiana Public Retirement System (INPRS) is among the largest 100 pension funds in the United States. We work to serve the needs of approximately 529,244 members representing 1,318 employers including public universities, school corporations, municipalities, and state agencies.

System Overview

The Indiana Public Retirement System (INPRS) is proud to publish our 2025 – 2027 strategic plan. This plan offers a clear strategy that lays out our vision for the future of INPRS and the members and employers we serve.

View Strategic Plan

Vision

Engaged members able to realize their retirement dreams.

Mission

As fiduciaries, educate stakeholders, collect necessary contributions, and prudently manage member assets to deliver promised DB and DC benefits and services.

Values

People are the foundation of our success. It takes people with different backgrounds, ideas, and strengths to be successful.
