Starting Your INPRS Career

A career with INPRS provides opportunities for growth, continuing education, collaboration, advancement, and comprehensive benefits packages, including:

  • An Annual Salary

  • Work/Life Balance (75 hours, bi-weekly pay period)

  • Flexible Hybrid Work Schedule w/ two remote and three in-office days per week

  • Holiday Time

  • Employer-funded Retirement Plan

  • Tuition Reimbursement & Professional Credentials

  • Competitive Medical/Dental/Vision Plans

  • Paid Leave (personal, sick & vacation, compensatory time)

  • Prescription Coverage

  • Health Savings Account with Employer Contribution

  • Flexible Spending Account

  • Optional 457 Plan with Employer Contribution

  • Parental Leave

  • Group Life Insurance

  • Short & Long-Term Disability

  • Free Downtown Parking
