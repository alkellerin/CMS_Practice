About INPRS

With approximately $50 billion in assets under management at fiscal year-end 2024, the Indiana Public Retirement System (INPRS) is among the largest 100 pension funds in the United States. We work to serve the needs of approximately 542,793 members representing 1,333 employers including public universities, school corporations, municipalities, and state agencies.
